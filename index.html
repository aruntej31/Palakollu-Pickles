Our Story

Every Family Has a Recipe

Every family has a recipe that carries more than flavor—it carries memories.

In the heart of Palakollu, where every meal is a celebration and every kitchen tells a story, our journey began with a simple belief: real food should taste like home.

Long before machines and mass production, our pickles were made the traditional way. Freshly harvested ingredients, cold-pressed sesame oil, hand-ground spices, and the patience to let every jar mature naturally. These weren't just recipes—they were family traditions lovingly passed from one generation to the next.

As time changed, many authentic flavors disappeared in the race for convenience. We refused to let that happen.

Palakollu Pickles was created to preserve the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh and share it with families across India. Every jar is handcrafted in small batches, using carefully selected ingredients and time-honored methods that respect tradition without compromising quality.

For us, a pickle is more than a side dish. It is the aroma that reminds you of your grandmother's kitchen, the taste that brings families together around the dining table, and the comfort of home—no matter where life takes you.

Today, every jar we send carries a piece of Palakollu with it. A story of heritage, craftsmanship, and love for authentic food.

Because some traditions deserve to be tasted, shared, and passed on—one jar at a time.

Why Palakollu

Why Choose Palakollu Pickles

Every jar is a promise — to use only what a grandmother in Palakollu would, and never anything she wouldn't.

Traditional Andhra Recipes

Passed down through generations of Palakollu kitchens, unchanged in spirit since the recipes were first written by hand.

Handmade in Small Batches

No factory lines. Each batch is mixed and matured by hand, in quantities small enough to watch over closely.

Premium Quality Ingredients

Farm-sourced mangoes, cold-pressed sesame oil, and hand-pounded spices — nothing enters a jar that we wouldn't serve our own family.

Rich Authentic Taste

The fire of red chilli, the tang of raw mango, the depth of slow maturation — flavor that can't be rushed or faked.

No Compromise on Quality

Zero artificial colors, zero shortcuts. If a batch isn't right, it doesn't leave the kitchen — let alone reach your table.

Secure Delivery Across India

Leak-proof sealing and careful packing mean your pickle arrives exactly as it left our kitchen — anywhere in India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our pickles stay fresh for up to 12 months unopened, and around 2–3 months once opened, if refrigerated and served with a dry spoon.

Orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours and typically arrive in 3–7 business days depending on your location across India.

Only farm-fresh produce, cold-pressed sesame or groundnut oil, and hand-pounded spices. No artificial colors or preservatives, ever.

Keep refrigerated after opening, always use a clean dry spoon, and ensure the oil layer covers the pickle to preserve freshness.
